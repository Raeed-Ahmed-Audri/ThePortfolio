My name is Raeed Ahmed Audri

In my view, an engineer embodies the essence of persistent resilience in the face of failure. These individuals or groups are made up of a series of setbacks, constantly experiencing failure in various tasks, projects, or calculations, yet never ceasing in their efforts. Engineers are distinguished by their unwavering perseverance, adaptability, and determination, regardless of the challenges they encounter. As I navigate my own journey in engineering, I acknowledge that I may falter; I may receive a low grade on a test, feel lost during work, or accidentally damage several capacitors. However, I will never stop trying, pursuing curiosity, working diligently, and learning from my mistakes until I ultimately achieve success. I humbly ask those reading this to grant me the opportunity to transform your company's setbacks into triumphs and, in the process, help me embody my definition of an engineer.

Career wise, the fields I someday hope to work in are Digital Signal Processing, RF Circuits, Control Systems, Power, and general consultation.
